About Pokemon unite:

Pokemon unite is a OMAG (Online Miltiplayer Arena Game) in where people around the world participate in something called unite battles in an arena which changes on special events. Pokemon unite is fast paced and is good for people who don't want to play the normal pokemon games, and its free to play! you can get the game on Nintendo Eshop (No game card) and mobile (Get on App Store). I recommend pokemon unite because of its special events like the 1st anniversary event which includes a map change in the standard and ranked matches, it also has a variety of pokemon to buy and battle with.
